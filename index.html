About Us

A Family Rooted in Colorado Soil

Sopris Party Rentals is a local, family-owned business serving the Roaring Fork Valley and surrounding communities. We proudly deliver to Glenwood Springs, Carbondale, Basalt, Aspen, Gypsum, Eagle, Silt, Newcastle, and Rifle.
