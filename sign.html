SOPRIS PARTY RENTALS — BOUNCE HOUSE RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Bounce House Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Sopris Party Rentals ("Company") and the Renter identified in the booking details above.

1. PAYMENT TERMS

The total rental fee is due in full on or before the day of the event. Sopris Party Rentals does not require a deposit at the time of booking.

2. CANCELLATION POLICY

Renter may cancel this Agreement without penalty with at least 24 hours notice prior to the scheduled event. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the event may result in a cancellation fee at the Company's discretion.

Sopris Party Rentals reserves the right to cancel any rental due to unsafe weather conditions including high winds exceeding 25 mph, lightning, or heavy rain. In the event of a Company-initiated weather cancellation, the Renter will be offered a full reschedule at no charge.

3. DELIVERY, SETUP & PICKUP

Sopris Party Rentals will deliver, set up, and retrieve the bounce house at the agreed times. The Renter agrees to ensure a clear, flat setup area is available and to be present at the event address at the agreed delivery time.

4. RULES OF USE

  • No shoes of any kind inside the bounce house.
  • No water, water guns, water balloons, or any liquid inside or around the bounce house.
  • No silly string, confetti, paint, or any substance that may damage the inflatable.
  • No sharp objects or jewelry worn inside the bounce house.
  • Minimum age of 3 years for all users.
  • No flipping, wrestling, or unsafe behavior inside the bounce house.
  • Maximum capacity must not be exceeded at any time.
  • Adult supervision is required at all times.

5. DAMAGE & EQUIPMENT RESPONSIBILITY

The Renter is responsible for the bounce house from setup until pickup. The Renter accepts financial responsibility for significant damage caused by misuse or negligence. Normal wear and tear including small pinholes are excluded from the Renter's responsibility.

6. ASSUMPTION OF RISK & LIABILITY

The Renter acknowledges that the use of inflatable bounce houses involves inherent risks of physical injury and assumes full responsibility for any injuries or incidents that occur during the rental period.

7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Colorado. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Garfield County, Colorado.
